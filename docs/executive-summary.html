Executive Summary

This analysis examines properties in the Episcopal Diocese of Virginia with fewer than 30 Sunday attendees, focusing on development potential through key metrics outlined in the analytical framework chapter. This report was done for VEREP (Virginia Episcopal Real Estate Partners) in conjunction with case study projects HDAdvisors is facilitating.

  • Urban/suburban areas with strong walkability scores
  • Markets with favorable demographics and LIHTC eligibility
  • Transit-accessible locations supporting reduced parking requirements
